The Jamroom is a Music and Audio Production House based out of Singapore and Mumbai, started by Rahul Pais and Nariman Khambata in 2015.

The Jamroom’s forte is creating original music and audio production for TVCs and Digital Content, Film, Radio, Games, Channel Branding and Animation. Our portfolio encompasses music and background scores, sound design, voiceover and language dubbing, and mix and master across projects.

At The Jamroom, we are passionate about our work and push ourselves and our boundaries creatively on every project. We strongly believe in working collaboratively with our clients to deliver quality results at cost-effective rates. Our goal is to build and maintain long-term relationships, and we strive towards customising packages built to work around every budget.
